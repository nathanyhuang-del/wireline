WELCOME TO WIRELINE ENGINEERING

What we do

We supply wireline equipment worldwide.

Wireline Engineering supplies wireline winches, wireline depth counter and associated products, manufactured in Perth, Western Australia. We are a market leader in Australia and worldwide in terms of functionality, performance, reliability and safety.

Our clients include Imdex, CSIRO, ABIM Solutions, Surtron Technologies, Age Developments, Surtech Systems, Independence Group NL, DHS, Poseidon Nickel, EMIT, GyroMax, Hiseis and Gyro Australia.

With our in-house research and development capabilities we are able to use the latest technology to enhance the functionality of our product range to ensure our clientele obtain best-in-breed solutions.

Products

We have many years of experience in wire line equipment design and manufacture. Our products include: Wireless Depth Tracker, Wireline Winches and Wireline Booms & Sheaves. We can also build custom design products to your specifications.

About Wireline Engineering

Wireline Winches and Equipment – Built for Performance, Reliability, and Safety

Established in 2010 in Perth, Western Australia, Wireline Engineering designs, manufactures, and supplies wireline winches, wireline depth counters, and related equipment to clients worldwide.

We are proud to be a market leader in Australia and across the globe, trusted for delivering equipment that combines cutting-edge technology, superior performance, and proven reliability.

With dedicated in-house research and development, we continuously innovate to enhance functionality and safety—ensuring our customers always receive best-in-class wireline solutions.

Products

We have many years of experience in wire line equipment design and manufacture. Our products include: Wireless Depth Tracker, Wireline Winches and Wireline Booms & Sheaves. We can also build custom design products to your specifications.

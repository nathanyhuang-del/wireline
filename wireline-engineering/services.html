Our Services

Vehicle Fitouts

Logging can be a demanding task and accordingly operator comfort is important to ensure the logging process is undertaken efficiently. Most of our clients require their logging equipment to resemble a mobile office.

Our vehicle fitouts typically include:

  • Special purpose boom
  • Rugged flooring
  • Operator seat and desks
  • Tool holders, cargo barriers etc.

Traditionally, the Toyota Landcruiser has been the most popular vehicle. However, due to stringent OH&S regulations imposed by mining and exploration companies in Australia, there has been a move towards Mitsubishi Canter and Isuzu Trucks.
